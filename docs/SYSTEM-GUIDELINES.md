# One Stop Food — System Guidelines

**Platform Policies, Standards & Compliance**  
Version 1.0 | Tanzania

---

## 1. Purpose

These System Guidelines define the rules, standards, and expectations for everyone using the One Stop Food platform — customers, chefs, travelers (droppers), and administrators. By registering or using the system, you agree to follow these guidelines in addition to the Terms of Service and Code of Conduct accepted during partner verification.

**Support:** support@onestopfood.co.tz

---

## 2. General Platform Rules

### 2.1 Eligibility

- You must be **18 years or older** to register as a chef or traveler.
- You must provide **accurate and truthful** information during registration and verification.
- One person may hold **one active account** per role unless approved by admin.

### 2.2 Account Security

- Do not share your login credentials.
- Admin accounts must use **strong passwords** and **2FA** when enabled.
- Social sign-in accounts (Google/Facebook) must complete **phone verification** and **OTP confirmation**.
- Report suspicious activity to support immediately.

### 2.3 Prohibited Conduct

The following are **strictly prohibited**:

- Fraud, fake orders, or payment manipulation
- Harassment, abuse, or discrimination toward any user
- Listing stolen, unsafe, or misrepresented food
- Operating without required licenses or permits
- Circumventing platform fees or direct off-platform deals solicited through the app
- Creating duplicate or impersonation accounts
- Uploading false verification documents

**Consequence:** Warning, suspension, or permanent removal from the platform.

---

## 3. Customer Guidelines

### 3.1 Ordering

- Provide a **correct delivery address** including city and district.
- Be available at the delivery location or ensure someone can receive the order.
- Pay promptly using the selected payment method.

### 3.2 Reviews

- Reviews must be **honest** and based on your actual experience.
- Do not use reviews for threats, hate speech, or unrelated complaints.
- Disputes about orders should go through support, not public harassment.

### 3.3 Cancellations

- Cancel only when necessary and as early as possible.
- Repeated frivolous cancellations may result in account restrictions.

---

## 4. Chef Guidelines

### 4.1 Professional Standards

Chefs on One Stop Food represent the platform brand. You must:

- Prepare food in a **clean, safe kitchen** environment.
- Use **fresh ingredients** and follow proper food handling practices.
- Accurately describe meals, ingredients, allergens, and portion sizes.
- Honor accepted orders within reasonable preparation time.
- Keep profile photos and meal images **current and truthful**.

### 4.2 Kitchen Requirements

| Requirement | Detail |
|-------------|--------|
| Kitchen type | Declare home kitchen, commercial kitchen, or shared facility |
| Proof of kitchen | Upload valid proof document |
| Kitchen photos | Minimum **2 photos** for home kitchens; show clean preparation areas |
| Location | Provide accurate street address, **city**, and **district** in Tanzania |

### 4.3 Legal & Compliance Documents

Chefs must upload valid documents where applicable:

- Food safety / hygiene certificate
- Business license
- Food handling permit
- Health inspection certificate
- Professional training certificates (optional but recommended)

**False documents lead to immediate rejection and possible permanent ban.**

### 4.4 Food Safety

- Maintain proper storage temperatures for perishable items.
- Avoid cross-contamination between raw and cooked foods.
- Do not sell expired or contaminated food.
- Report illness-related incidents to support immediately.

### 4.5 Payouts

- Provide accurate **bank account details** for earnings.
- Platform fees and payment schedules are defined in admin configuration.
- Earnings are tied to **delivered and paid** orders.

---

## 5. Traveler / Dropper Guidelines

### 5.1 Delivery Standards

Travelers must:

- Treat food with care — use insulated bags where possible.
- Follow traffic laws and drive safely.
- Deliver to the **address on the order** only.
- Confirm delivery with the customer when required.
- Maintain a professional appearance and communication.

### 5.2 Vehicle & Documentation

| Document | Required |
|----------|----------|
| Valid driving license | Yes |
| Vehicle photo | Yes |
| Proof of vehicle ownership or authorization | Yes |
| Vehicle insurance | Yes |
| Proof of address | Yes |

### 5.3 Availability

- Toggle **online/offline** status honestly.
- Accept deliveries only when you can complete them promptly.
- Do not delay orders without updating status or contacting support.

### 5.4 Cash on Delivery (COD)

- Collect the **exact amount** shown on the order.
- Do not request extra fees outside the platform.
- Report payment disputes through the admin dispute process.

## 6. Verification & Approval Process

### 6.1 How It Works

1. User registers as **Chef** or **Traveler**.
2. User completes the **multi-step verification form** (`/verify`).
3. Documents are synced to the admin **Verifications** queue.
4. Admin reviews identity, documents, and profile completeness.
5. Admin **approves**, **rejects**, or **requests more information**.

### 6.2 Approval Criteria

Admin approval is granted when:

- All required fields are complete.
- Identity documents are clear and readable.
- Selfie matches identity expectations.
- Kitchen / vehicle evidence is sufficient.
- No red flags in declarations or background consent responses.

### 6.3 Rejection & Re-application

- Rejected users may **update their verification profile** and resubmit.
- Repeated rejections for false information may result in permanent ban.

## 7. Order & Delivery Policy

### 7.1 Order Lifecycle

```
Customer places order
    → Chef accepts/rejects
    → Chef prepares (status updates)
    → Order ready
    → Traveler assigned & picks up
    → Out for delivery
    → Delivered
    → Customer may review
```

### 7.2 Responsibilities

| Stage | Responsible party |
|-------|-------------------|
| Order accuracy | Customer |
| Food quality & preparation | Chef |
| Timely pickup & delivery | Traveler |
| Payment processing | Platform + payment provider |
| Disputes | Admin |

### 7.3 Cancellations & Refunds

- Admins may cancel orders and process refunds through the **Finance** module.
- Refund eligibility depends on order status and payment method.
- Contact support@onestopfood.co.tz for unresolved payment issues.

## 8. Data Privacy & Documents

### 8.1 Personal Data

The platform collects:

- Name, email, phone number
- Identity and verification documents
- Location and delivery addresses
- Order and payment history
- Login activity (IP, device) for security

### 8.2 Document Handling

- Verification documents are stored securely and accessed only by authorized admins.
- Selfies and kitchen/vehicle photos are used **only** for verification and platform safety.
- Do not share other users' personal information outside the platform.

### 8.3 Social Sign-In Data

When you sign in with Google or Facebook, the platform receives:

- Name and email (from provider)
- Profile photo URL (optional)

Passwords are **never** stored for social-only accounts.

---

## 9. Admin & Moderation

### 9.1 Admin Powers

Administrators may:

- Approve or reject partner applications
- Suspend or unsuspend user accounts
- Monitor orders, payments, and logistics
- Resolve disputes
- Export user data for compliance
- Configure platform settings and delivery zones

### 9.2 Account Suspension & Deletion

| Action | When used |
|--------|-----------|
| **Suspend** | Policy violations, fraud suspicion, repeated complaints |
| **Reject** | Incomplete or invalid verification |
| **Bulk delete** | Only accounts with **no orders, meals, reviews, or disputes**; otherwise suspend is recommended |

Admins must use **Suspend/Block** instead of delete when a user has order history linked to the platform.

### 9.3 Impersonation

- Admins may impersonate users **only** for legitimate support and debugging.
- Impersonation activity is logged.

## 10. Communication Standards

### 10.1 Platform Communications

- Order updates are shown in the dashboard.
- Email confirmations are sent from **noreply@onestopfood.co.tz**.
- Support inquiries go to **support@onestopfood.co.tz**.

### 10.2 Contacting Support

Include in your message:

- Your registered email
- Role (customer, chef, traveler)
- Order number (if applicable)
- Clear description of the issue

**Response time:** Support aims to respond within 1–2 business days.
